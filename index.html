About Lukas

Lukas Stojanov is an accomplished professional with a robust background in engineering, business development, and strategic partnerships. He holds degrees in Software Engineering from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology and Mechanical Engineering from UNSW. Further enhancing his expertise, Lukas completed a certification in “Mastering Negotiation and Influence” from MIT xPRO, showcasing his skills in strategic influence and deal closure.

Currently based in Paris with his family, Lukas serves as Cryolock distributor in partnership Caring IVF, focusing on cryopreservation sales across Southern Europe. He is also the founder of Open-IVF, leveraging his technical and commercial acumen to drive innovative solutions in the fertility sector, launching successful products, and generating significant revenue growth, Lukas is committed to advancing cutting-edge solutions in healthcare and beyond.
